The Super Slap Hand is a post-moonlord melee weapon that hits slow, deals low damage,
but has a really high knockback value.

It is similar to the Slap Hand, but hits slower, deals more damage, and knocks back harder.
To craft it, you need 15 Zenithite bars at the Ancient Manipulator.

Stats and Usage

The Super Slap Hand deals 600 damage, is melee, has a size of 34 x 34,
has a use time of 19, use animation time of 19, and a knockback value
of 100. It is a red rarity item.

Use the Super Slap Hand to push away enemies when they get too close!
